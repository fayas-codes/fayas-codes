The objective of this project was to gather a list of 30 taxi services in Las Vegas with their respective important information like Phone, Email, Website and Adress. The contents should be structured neatly and ready for industrial use.
The tools i used are web scraper and google sheers. I extracted the information from the website with a scraper tool and formatted it and cleared our errors and repetitivity by using google sheets and double verifying.
The result was a perfect table with no errors and absent variables marked N/A for the ease of viewing.
